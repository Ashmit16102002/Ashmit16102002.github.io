About Me

I am currently a Master's student in Mathematics at the Kerala School of Mathematics, Kozhikode, India. I completed my undergraduate studies in Mathematics and Computing at the Institute of Mathematics and Applications, Bhubaneswar, graduating in mid-2024.

My research interests lie in the study of inverse problems in the realm of X-ray Tomography. Earlier I have worked on the Image reconstruction of the Radon transform and its applications in computerized tomography.

I am currently working on my Master Thesis under the guidance of Prof. Subrahmanyam Gorthi.

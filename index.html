This is me.

Ashmit Gurey is a mathematics student currently pursuing his Master's at the Kerala School of Mathematics, Kozhikode, India. His research interests lie in the study of inverse problems and X-ray tomography, with a focus on the Radon transform and its applications in image reconstruction.

He completed his undergraduate studies in Mathematics and Computing at the Institute of Mathematics and Applications, Bhubaneswar. He is currently working on his Master’s Thesis under the supervision of Prof. Subrahmanyam Gorthi.

When not working on mathematics, Ashmit enjoys reading, exploring creative ideas, and developing interactive academic tools. He’s always looking forward to collaborating on interesting problems in imaging and applied analysis.
